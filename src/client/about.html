About Us
Who are we

We are a leading sports nutrition brand, supplying a range of high-quality products, including protein powder, vitamins and minerals, high-protein foods and snack alternatives. Every day we work to inspire people of all ages and genders to believe in their fitness potential, then incentivize them to achieve it

What we do

At INOVA, our goal is to spark the ambitions of people around the world - making the best of sports nutrition for everyone, regardless of their purpose. We pride ourselves on offering a wide selection of products of exceptional value to make this possible, including a range of nutritional needs including vegetarian, vegan, dairy-free and gluten-free, so that every customer can enjoy the benefits of high-quality nutrition.

Sustainability and community

INOVA is passionate about fueling the potential of people regardless of gender, location or background. As a global company, we are home to more than 70 nationalities and embrace this diversity.

As INOVA has continued to grow and diversify, this also applies to our range and we nowalso specialize in a selection of plant and organic products. This vegan range has expanded into one of our most popular offerings. We now supply an extensive variety of products that do not contain animal substances, so we can play a role in improving overall animal welfare and reducing toxic emissions.
